1. New York Hot Dog

The quintessential American street food, New York-style hot dogs are steamed or grilled sausages served in a soft bun. Toppings vary from mustard and sauerkraut to the famous "dirty water dog."

2. Texas BBQ

Authentic Texas barbecue features slow-smoked meats like brisket, ribs, and sausage, cooked over hardwood fires. The emphasis is on the meat itself with a simple salt and pepper rub.

3. Tex-Mex Tacos

A fusion of Mexican and American cuisines, Tex-Mex tacos feature tortillas filled with seasoned beef, lettuce, cheese, and tomatoes. Unlike traditional Mexican tacos, these often include cheddar and sour cream.

4. Philly Cheesesteak

This iconic sandwich features thinly sliced beefsteak, melted cheese, and onions on a hoagie roll. It was invented in Philadelphia and remains a beloved street food.

5. Lobster Roll

A New England specialty, the lobster roll features fresh lobster meat in a grilled hot dog bun, either warm with butter or chilled with mayo.
