Belgian Waffles

Delightfully crisp outside and tender inside, served with sugar or chocolate.

Ingredients:
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 eggs, separated
  • 1 3/4 cups milk
  • 1/2 cup melted butter
Steps:
  1. Mix flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  2. Whisk egg yolks, milk, and melted butter.
  3. Fold in egg whites beaten to stiff peaks.
  4. Cook in a preheated waffle iron until golden.
  5. Top with sugar, chocolate, fruits, or whipped cream.

Italian Arancini

Golden, crispy on the outside and soft, cheesy on the inside, Arancini are Sicilian rice balls stuffed with rag√π, mozzarella, or peas.

Ingredients:
  • 2 cups cooked risotto rice
  • 1/2 cup mozzarella, cubed
  • 1/2 cup peas (optional)
  • 1/2 cup cooked meat rag√π (optional)
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs
  • Vegetable oil for frying
Steps:
  1. Take a small portion of rice, flatten it in your hand.
  2. Place a cube of mozzarella and some rag√π/peas in the center.
  3. Enclose filling with rice, shaping a ball.
  4. Dip in beaten egg, then coat with breadcrumbs.
  5. Fry in hot oil until golden brown.
  6. Drain on paper towels and serve warm.

Spanish Churros

Deep-fried, sugar-coated dough sticks, crunchy outside, soft inside. Served with thick hot chocolate.

Ingredients:
  • 1 cup water
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • Oil for frying
  • 1/2 cup sugar + 1 tsp cinnamon for coating
Steps:
  1. Boil water, sugar, salt, and oil together.
  2. Remove from heat, stir in flour until smooth dough forms.
  3. Pipe dough into hot oil in 4-6 inch strips.
  4. Fry until golden brown.
  5. Drain, then roll in cinnamon sugar.
  6. Serve with warm chocolate sauce.

German Bratwurst

Savory sausage, grilled and served in a bun with mustard or sauerkraut.

Ingredients:
  • 1 lb ground pork
  • 1/4 lb veal or beef (optional)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp white pepper
  • 1/2 tsp nutmeg
  • 1/2 tsp marjoram
  • Sausage casings
Steps:
  1. Mix meat with spices thoroughly.
  2. Stuff into casings and twist into individual sausages.
  3. Grill until cooked through and golden.
  4. Serve in a bun with mustard or sauerkraut.

French Crepes

Thin pancakes, sweet or savory, filled with Nutella, fruit, ham, or cheese.

Ingredients:
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tbsp melted butter
  • Pinch of salt
Steps:
  1. Whisk all ingredients until smooth.
  2. Heat a non-stick pan and lightly grease.
  3. Pour batter thinly, cook 1-2 min per side.
  4. Fill with desired toppings and fold elegantly.

Greek Souvlaki

Marinated grilled meat on skewers or pita, with tzatziki, tomatoes, and onions.

Ingredients:
  • 1 lb pork or chicken, cubed
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp oregano
  • Salt and pepper
  • Pita bread, vegetables, tzatziki for serving
Steps:
  1. Marinate meat 2-4 hours.
  2. Skewer and grill until cooked through.
  3. Serve in pita with vegetables and tzatziki.

Portuguese Pastel de Nata

Creamy egg custard tart in flaky pastry, lightly caramelized on top.

Ingredients:
  • 1 sheet puff pastry
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 tbsp cornstarch
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 3 egg yolks
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
Steps:
  1. Roll pastry into tart molds.
  2. Cook milk, sugar, cornstarch until thick.
  3. Temper in egg yolks and vanilla.
  4. Pour into pastry and bake at high heat until caramelized.

Polish Zapiekanka

Open-faced baguette pizza, toasted with mushrooms, cheese, and ketchup or garlic sauce.

Ingredients:
  • Baguette slices
  • Butter
  • Mushrooms, sliced
  • Grated cheese
  • Ketchup or garlic sauce
Steps:
  1. Toast baguette slices lightly.
  2. Saut√© mushrooms in butter.
  3. Top bread with mushrooms and cheese.
  4. Broil until cheese melts.
  5. Drizzle with sauce and serve.

Swedish Cinnamon Bun

Sweet, soft, spiced bun, often with cardamom, topped with pearl sugar.

Ingredients:
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 tsp ground cardamom
  • 1 package yeast
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • Filling: butter, sugar, cinnamon
Steps:
  1. Prepare yeast dough with milk, butter, sugar, flour, and cardamom.
  2. Roll dough, spread filling, roll and cut into buns.
  3. Let rise, then bake until golden.
  4. Sprinkle with pearl sugar and serve warm.

Hungarian Langos

Deep-fried flatbread topped with sour cream, cheese, or garlic butter.

Ingredients:
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 package yeast
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 cup warm water
  • Oil for frying
  • Toppings: sour cream, grated cheese, garlic
Steps:
  1. Mix flour, yeast, salt, and warm water into a soft dough.
  2. Let rise for 1 hour.
  3. Divide, shape into flat discs.
  4. Fry in hot oil until golden.
  5. Top with sour cream, cheese, or garlic butter.
